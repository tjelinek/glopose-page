Abstract

[Draft placeholder — to be replaced with the paper abstract.] GloPose is a model-free 6DoF object pose estimation system following the BOP benchmark paradigm. From a video or image stream of a novel object, it produces a posed 3D reconstruction and a detection-ready representation, which are then used to detect the object and estimate its 6DoF pose in novel images. At the core of the onboarding stage is dense matching, used both for adaptive keyframe selection and for establishing correspondences that drive structure-from-motion reconstruction.
